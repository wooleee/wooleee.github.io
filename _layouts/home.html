This is Woo Chul Lee,a master's student in Applied Machine Learning Laboratory of Seoul National University Graduate School of Data Science. I'm advised by Prof. Wen-Syan Li. My research interests are 'Causal Inference for Fairness in Machine Learning' and 'Applied Machine Learning in Financial Services'. Before graduate school, I was an assistant manager in GS Caltex Corporation Customs Tax team. During my career as FTA analyst, I worked on two FTA litigations projects(KR-EU and KR-US FTA) and one SAP based IT project called FTA OMS(Origin Management System), as a project manager. I received bachelors degree in Sungkyunkwan University Business School, majoring in Global Business and Finance. As CFA Charterholder and Data Science researcher, I am eager to make AI good for human being, while AI and human complement each other, in all realm.
